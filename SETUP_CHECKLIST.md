## Phase 4: 📌 DO THIS WEEK (30 mins)

### Step 1: Buy Domain
- [ ] Decide domain name (e.g., swiftpackpro.com)
- [ ] Check availability at https://whois.com
- [ ] Choose registrar (Namecheap, Google Domains, etc.)
- [ ] Buy domain (typically $8-12/year)
- [ ] Verify email confirmation

### Step 2: Connect Domain to Vercel
- [ ] Go to Vercel project dashboard
- [ ] Click **Settings** → **Domains**
- [ ] Enter your domain
- [ ] Click **Add**
- [ ] Copy DNS records shown

### Step 3: Update DNS in Registrar
- [ ] Log into registrar (Namecheap, etc.)
- [ ] Go to DNS Settings
- [ ] Add the records Vercel provided
- [ ] Save

### Step 4: Wait & Verify
- [ ] Wait 24-48 hours for DNS propagation
- [ ] Visit https://dnschecker.org
- [ ] Enter your domain
- [ ] Once all green ✅ → Visit your domain
- [ ] Your site is LIVE! 🎉
